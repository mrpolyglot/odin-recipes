Egg Salad

Ingredients

  • 6 hard-cooked eggs, chopped
  • 1/4 cup chopped cucumber
  • 3 tablespoons ranch dressing
  • 1 tablesppoon mustard
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat pan.
  2. Add 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil.
  3. Combine eggs, cucumber, ranch dressing, mustard, salt, and black pepper in a bowl.
  4. Enjoy your meal!
