Tunisian Soup

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 cup couscous
  • 8 cups water
  • 2 zucchini, chopped
  • 1 potato, chopped
  • 20 green beans, chopped
  • 2 carrots, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons tomato puree
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chile powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. Preheat pan.
  2. Melt butter in a pot over medium heat.
  3. Add onion.
  4. Cook and stir until translucent, about 5 minutes.
  5. Mix in couscous
  6. Cook and stir until browned, about 5 minutes.
  7. Stir in water, zucchini, potato, green beans, carrots, tomato puree, coriander, chile powder, cumin, and salt
  8. Bring to a boil.
  9. Simmer until tender, about 20 minutes.
  10. Stir in milk.
